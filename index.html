About Myself

Digital developer with a passion for structured development, automation and thoughtful UX design for both users and admins.

I’ve always sought out opportunities that are both meaningful and challenging to me. Although my professional path has taken me through different projects and industries — from sport and governing bodies, to e-commerce and membership, to small business owner and entrepreneur — I've never stopped engaging my passion to solve problems and implement lasting solutions.

As a web developer, I enjoy using my problem solving skills and strong teamwork ability to help deliver projects and goals on time and with lasting effects and results. That's why I’m excited to make a big impact at a high growth company.
